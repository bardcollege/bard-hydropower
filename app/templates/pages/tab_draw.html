Find a location for a potential micro-hydro installation.

Use the map to the right, the search tool, and the available layers to find locations suitable for microhydro installation. Streams and weir locations are available as map layers. Switch the basemap between several available imagery and topographic to get a better sense of the land.

Sketch a conceptual installation.

Use the draw tool to draw a line from a point downstream (lower elevation) to a point upstream (higher elevation) to indicate the potential location of a micro-hydro installation.

Use the edit button to modify your sketch.
